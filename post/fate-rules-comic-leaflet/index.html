A comic book tutorial for Fate Core

Written by David Teller with ♥ on September 21, 2018 in rpg tutorial ronin-time-bureaucrats

If you are interested in improv/narrative role-playing games, you may have heard of Fate Core, a great ruleset made by Evil Hat exactly for this purpose. It’s simple, it’s excellent, and it’s missing a tutorial, so here is one:

As you can see, it fits in one sheet, and it’s designed to be printed and brought with you at your table.

I’d like to make it available in other languages – if you wish to help, don’t hesitate to get in touch!

Also available as a high-resolution pdf.

Fate Core is available as pay-what-you-want, so don’t hesitate to take a look at the book!

This tutorial was initially developed as part of Ronin Time Bureaucrats.

Credit for the idea of a Fate Core summary of rules goes to Up to 4 players.
