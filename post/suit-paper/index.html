Space·Time Deck: The Paper Suit

Written by David Teller with ♥ on October 11, 2018 in rpg ronin-time-bureaucrats improv space-time-deck plotonomicon

A few weeks ago, we introduced the Trump Cards of the Space·Time Deck. It’s time for a second delivery: the Paper Suit.

Paper traditionally symbolizes: - knowledge and wisdom; - riches; - fragility.

The cards: - Ruler

- Dragon - Treasure - Soldier - Builder - Seeker - Lover - Servant - Home - Foot (blank, customizable) - Teddy bear (blank, customizable)

Note that the blue borders are not part of the cards. They are just there to delimit the safe-to-draw zone. Printed cards should have a little white margin between the part you can see above and the border.
